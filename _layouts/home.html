Howdy! I'm Rob, an engineer, aspiring paragliding pilot, and avid outdoorsman.

I currently work as a software engineer at Reliable Robotics, developing Detect and Avoid (DAA) solutions for unmanned aircraft in the national airspace. I compete (or have competed) in the Northern California Cross Country League and the xRedRocks. In 2021, I completed thru-hikes of the Pacific Crest Trail and the Long Trail.

What you'll find here:

Projects:
Flying Better: Thoughts and theories on competition and cross country unpowered flight.
Outdoor Gear: Write ups from outdoor gear that I've made for myself and for my small business Merlin Gear.
Micro Soaring: Development log for Micro Soaring, a multiplayer browser game I'm writing to refine my gliding tactics and fly on poor weather days

Trip Reports: Selections of writing of my experiences & misadventures around California and wherever else I end up.

Thoughts and Notes: Occasionally I write bits and pieces of things down that don't fit in the other categories.
